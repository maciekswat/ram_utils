Summary data useful for selecting stimulation targets is provided for each bipolar electrode in a subject's montage. For the 'p-value' and 't-stat' fields, high frequency activity (68, 110, 180 Hz) was measured across the word presentation interval (0 to 1600ms). At each electrode, a t-test compared HFA for subsequently recalled vs subsequently forgotten items. P-values are FDR-corrected. Red - significant positive effect (subsequently remembered HFA > subsequently forgotten HFA). Blue - significant negative effect (subsequently remembered HFA < subsequently forgotten HFA). Black - difference not significant. All bipolar pairs are shown in descending order of significance. When data is available, an additional column "controllability" represents the modal controllability based on the Basset lab's DTI processing pipeline.
